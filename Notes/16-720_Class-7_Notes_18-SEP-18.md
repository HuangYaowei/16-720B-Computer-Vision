### Pin Hole Cameras
- These images are flipped (UD/LR) due to rays crossing each other
- Optical center is always at the origin
- x/f = u/w = tan(theta-x)
- y/f = v/w = tan(theta-y)
- Focal length affects the FOV, inversely 
- Aperture controls the blur
